Introduction

This PC is designed to deliver high performance on all of today's most popular games without being complicated to build or costing too much. It will run well at high settings on 1080p or higher when playing Valorant, Fortnite, Minecraft, Call of Duty, Rainbow Six Siege, Apex Legends, and more.

CPU

The AMD Ryzen 5 7600 provides some of the best bang for your buck. It offers 6 cores, 12 threads, a base frequency of 3.8GHz, and a turbo frequency of 5.1GHz. For better cooling, we've included the Thermalright Peerless Assassin. If you'd prefer to consider a similar build using an Intel processor, be sure to check out our Great Intel Gaming Build Guide.

GPU

The Nvidia GeForce RTX 3060 Ti is a great choice for this build. It offers 8GB of GDDR6 memory, 4864 CUDA cores, and a boost clock of 1665MHz. It's a great choice for 1080p and 1440p gaming.

RAM

For RAM, we've chosen the Corsair Vengeance LPX 16GB kit. It offers 2x8GB sticks of DDR4 memory with a speed of 3200MHz. This is a great choice for gaming and multitasking.

Storage

For storage, we've included the Samsung 970 EVO Plus 500GB NVMe SSD. This drive offers read speeds of up to 3500MB/s and write speeds of up to 3300MB/s. It's a great choice for your operating system and most-used programs.

PSU

The Corsair RM750x is a great choice for this build. It offers 750W of power, 80+ Gold certification, and fully modular cables. It's a great choice for this build and leaves room for future upgrades.

Case

The NZXT H510 is a great choice for this build. It offers a sleek design, tempered glass side panel, and plenty of room for cable management. It's a great choice for this build and offers room for future upgrades.
